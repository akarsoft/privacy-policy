ListingLedger

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 13, 2026

ListingLedger ("we," "our," or "us") is an IRS-compliant expense tracking application designed for real estate professionals. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and related services.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address
  • First and last name
  • Authentication credentials (managed via Apple Sign In or email-based authentication through Amazon Cognito)

1.2 Business Profile Information

To provide tax-compliant expense tracking, we collect:

  • Business name and EIN (Employer Identification Number)
  • Home office address and square footage
  • Vehicle information (make, model, year)
  • Tax year and accounting method preferences

1.3 Financial and Transaction Data

When you connect your bank accounts through Plaid, we collect:

  • Transaction amounts, dates, and merchant names
  • Transaction descriptions and categories
  • Institution name

We store an encrypted Plaid access token to maintain your bank connection. We do not store your bank login credentials.

1.4 Receipt and Expense Data

When you scan or upload receipts, we collect:

  • Receipt images
  • Extracted text via on-device OCR processing
  • Merchant names, amounts, dates, and line items
  • AI-generated expense classifications

1.5 Location and Mileage Data

With your permission, we collect location data to track business mileage:

  • GPS coordinates (start and end points of trips)
  • Route information and distance traveled
  • Trip timestamps and duration
  • Trip purpose and business descriptions

Location tracking requires your explicit "Always Allow" permission and can be disabled at any time through your device settings.

1.6 Property and Client Information

For listing management, we collect:

  • Property addresses and MLS numbers
  • Client names, email addresses, and phone numbers
  • Commission amounts and listing details

1.7 Subscription Information

Subscription purchases are processed through Apple's App Store. We receive subscription status and tier information but do not process or store payment card details.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the expense tracking service
  • Generate IRS Schedule C-compliant tax reports
  • Automatically categorize and track business expenses
  • Calculate mileage deductions using IRS standard rates
  • Analyze receipts and classify expenses
  • Sync financial transactions from connected bank accounts
  • Track business gift limits for tax compliance
  • Send transactional emails related to your account
  • Process and manage your subscription

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to provide our functionality:

Service Purpose Data Shared
Plaid Bank account connection and transaction sync Bank credentials (processed by Plaid, not stored by us)
Amazon Web Services (AWS) Cloud infrastructure, authentication, data storage All user data is stored on AWS (Cognito, DynamoDB, S3, Lambda)

Each third-party service operates under its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review their respective policies.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: Core data is stored on your device using SwiftData for offline access.
  • Cloud Storage: Data is synced and stored on AWS infrastructure (DynamoDB for structured data, S3 for files).
  • Encryption: Authentication tokens use RS256 encryption. Plaid access tokens are stored encrypted.
  • Receipt Images: Uploaded to secure AWS S3 storage.
  • Tax Reports: Generated PDF reports are stored with automatic expiration (90 days).

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide our services. When you delete your account:

  • Your data is marked for deletion and removed from active use
  • Receipt images and generated reports are deleted from storage
  • Bank connections through Plaid are disconnected

We may retain certain data as required by tax regulations or legal obligations.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format
  • Location Control: Enable or disable location tracking at any time through your device settings
  • Bank Disconnect: Remove connected bank accounts at any time within the app

California Residents (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act, including the right to know what personal information we collect, the right to delete your data, and the right to opt out of the sale of your personal information. We do not sell your personal information.

7. Children's Privacy

ListingLedger is designed for business professionals and is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us so we can promptly remove it.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated policy within the app and updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this page. Your continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
